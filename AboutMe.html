Todd Law everything you ever wanted to know about me!

Todd's Website

by Todd Law

Posted on March 2, 2017 at 11:20 AM

I was born in Reynoldsburg Ohio and graduated from The Ohio State University with a Bachelors of Science in Computer Information Sciences.

I have spent decades working as a Mainframe programmer mainly in COBOL, CICS, DB/2, JCL, VSAM, and Assembler using TSO/ISPF, Changeman, Quality Center, Debug, and Fault Analyzer. I also have experience using XML, QMF, IMS, and PL/I.

For the last 20 years I have lived in Victorian Village long before it was the cool part of town! I spent quite a bit of time working on different non-profit boards like the Short North Neighborhood Foundation that raised the money to put the arches over High Street. I spent 10 years on the board of the Friends of Goodale Park and helped get the new fountain built in the pond there. I was the Treasurer and a board member of the Columbus Blues Alliance which brings the Traditional Blues Festival to town every year. I was Secretary of the Victorian Village Society, and Secretary of the Spring-Sandusky Interchange task force that fought for years to lessen the impact of the yet to be built I670 freeway on our Federally Protected Historic Neighborhood too. Currently I am the BeerMiester that runs the after party for the DooDah Parade every 4th of July in the Short North and last year we raised over $10,000 for charity!
